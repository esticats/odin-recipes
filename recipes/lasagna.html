Lasagna Recipe

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • An egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
